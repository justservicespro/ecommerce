Five shops. One quiet aesthetic.

Why a network

Each shop is a self-contained demo — its own palette, type, and layout system, sharing one cart and checkout engine underneath.
"Each shop genuinely feels different — not just a recolored template. The furniture store alone could pass for a real boutique site."
— Demo Reviewer, Worn & Found
"Checkout flow is smooth and the confirmation screen is a nice touch for a prototype. Cart persists across sessions too."
— Demo Reviewer, Circuit & Sound
"Useful as a portfolio piece — five distinct visual directions built on one shared system, all static and easy to host."
— Demo Reviewer, Greenleaf Market
